App Features

Discover how Red Hope makes blood donation simple and effective

Create Profile

Easily create and manage your donor profile with personal information and blood group details.

Location-based Search

Find nearby blood donors in real-time based on your current location or specified area.

Donation Tracking

Track your last donation date with countdown timer and maintain your donation history.

Secure Authentication

Your data is protected with secure Firebase authentication and Firestore rules.

Emergency Contact

How Red Hope Works

Our intuitive interface makes it easy to find donors, request blood, and manage your donation activities.

  • Create and edit your donor profile
  • Search for donors by location and blood type
  • Track your donation history and schedule
  • Request blood in emergency situations
  • Earn badges for your contributions

Built with Java, Firebase, and XML for a seamless user experience.
